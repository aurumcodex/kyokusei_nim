=== Controls
.Control Scheme
[%header,cols=2]
|===================================================
| Button            | Input
| D-Pad Left        | Move Left
| D-Pad Right       | Move Right
| D-Pad Up          | `unused`
| D-Pad Down        | `unused`
| A Button          | Invert Gravity
| B Button          | Shoot Projectile
| L Button          | Shift to Impulse Forme
| R Button          | Shift to Keen Forme
| SELECT Button     | Change to Era/Echo
| START Button      | `unused`
|===================================================
